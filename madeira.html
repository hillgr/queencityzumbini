Osceola Drive Private Zumbini Class
Mondays 5:00 pm - 5:45 pm
June 21 - August 2 (no class July 5)

Join us for a 6-week Zumbini session! This is a private group just for the friends on Osceola Drive. Sing, dance, play instruments, and bond with your child as you wind down at the end of the day with your friends and neighbors. All supplies are provided, but if you feel more comfortable bringing your own, that is fine, as well! More information will be sent out prior to classes beginning.

Once you register, expect an email to follow in the weeks before the class with more details about how we will keep everyone safe in class through social distancing and other measures. If you have any questions feel free to email queencityzbini@gmail.com
