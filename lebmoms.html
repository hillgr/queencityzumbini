Queen City Zumbini welcomes the Lebanon Moms Group to a private Zumbini class in the Park! Sing, dance, play instruments, and more in a socially distant, safe outdoor setting. Queen City Zumbini has been hosting socially distant outdoor Zumbini classes for the past few weeks and they have all been safely handled and given families a nice reprieve from the cabin fever we have all been experiencing.

Join us on Friday, October 2 at 10:00 am for a 45 minute class. You will find us at Armco Park near Pavilion 32 (Located next to the baseball fields off of Greentree Rd). It's only $8 per child (any additional children under 6 months old are free with an older sibling).

Once you register, expect an email to follow in the weeks before the class with more details about how we will keep everyone safe in class through social distancing and other measures. If you have any questions feel free to email queencityzbini@gmail.com
