Saturday, August 27th @ 10:30 AM
Blue Ash Nature Park
4433 Cooper Road

(Find us in the shady, grassy area)

This class is sponsored by the Down Syndrome Association of Greater Cincinnati (DSAGC) 2020 Birth Year Group. Queen City Zumbini is thrilled to partner with this wonderful organization to bring you a 45-minute class full of singing, dancing, and fun! Zumbini was created for children from newborns up to age 4 or 5 (and beyond!) and for all abilities. In class we will use original music that has influences from all around the world to sing, dance, play instruments, and more. It's an upbeat class that is sure to be a great start to your day!

Please bring a blanket or towel to sit on in the grass. We recommend wearing layers in case you get warm. Please bring a water for you and your child. Remember to take a potty break and/or diaper change prior to class. Safety and health are a priority in our classes, so if you or your child are not feeling well please let us know and we will find another class for you to join when you are feeling better. All items used in class are sanitized prior to each use. Feel free to reach out to queencityzbini@gmail.com with any questions.
